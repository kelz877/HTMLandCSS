How did I get here?

I am from Texas originally and then my family moved to the UK when I was 10 years old. When I was 13 years old, we moved to Cairo, Egypt. I graduated from a US high school in Cairo and attended Santa Clara University for my undergrad. I would definitel have called myself "lost" during my late teens and early twenties and I wish I had taken a gap year to figure out what I wanted to do. I ended up double majoring in Political Science and Psychology and knew I was not going to be abel to find a job in the Bay Area. So after graduation, I packed up and moved to Denver, Colorado where I worked in a few different fields. I started working in youth corrections, as that was the only job someone with a Bachelors in Political Science and Psychology could get. I hated it. So I went to work as an Oracle Business Analyst at a lumber copmany. I liked the work and the environment but thought that I needed to be making more of a difference in the world. So I went to grad school and got my Masters in Counseling Psychology. I worked as a school counselor for 5 years and became burnt out on education.
