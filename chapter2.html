How did I get here?

I lived in Denver, Colorado for 10 years and wanted a change. I was feeling unfulfilled at work and like I was not reaching my full potential. I was googling my grandmother who is a decently well-known artist in some circles and I was shocked at the state of her website. I realized that I thought I could help her build something better and began learning how to try and do that. The best way for me to help out was to enroll in a bootcamp and change the path my life was going down. I was struggling learning on my own and found DigitalCrafts in Houston and decided that I wanted to make the jump.
